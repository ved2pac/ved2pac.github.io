ABOUT

Vedang is an initiator and the one who get things done. He is currently a graduate student at IIT Kanpur and works as a freelance consultant, marketer and a public speaker on the subject of leadership and team management. He has worked with/ recommendations from Jack Allison (Screenwriter for Academy Awards - Oscars, 68th Primetime Emmy Awards), Director - Omidyar Network, Dell Services, Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited, EY (Ernst and Young), CPA+ Israel, etc. and has published 10 manuscripts in various national and international journals/ conferences along with 3 article publications.

Vedang is a recipient of many national and international awards and recognitions by Kshitij Marwah (Head of MIT Media Labs, India), Alberto Minetti (Nobel Laureate) and organizations including Pollution Control Board, Harvard Project for Asian and International Relations by Harvard University, etc. He has always felt the hunger to achieve more and love to master new skills with an idea of expanding his domain of expertise and knowledge. He loves to discuss ideas and appreciate honest feedback.
