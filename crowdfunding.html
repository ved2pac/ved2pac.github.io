INSIGHTS FROM METHODOLOGY

The word "Design" gets thrown around a lot. To put it simply, design (in the digital space) is the process of making something  — useful that  also — looks great. Designing for the web and for mobile applications is a blend of art and science. Designers at Paper Tiger are experienced and educated in best practices for usability, interaction, and aesthetic.

We closely follow trends in our industry and always aim to get ahead of them. Before we start a new design project, we ask and answer the following questions in a series of exercises that may include deliverables such as Wireframes, Outline, and/or a Competitive Analysis.

Human Centered

WHO will be using the finished product?

Business Oriented

WHAT can we learn from your competitors?

Time Responsible

WHEN does the work need to be completed?

Gamification

HOW WILL THE DESIGN  ENGAGE THE USER?

Solution Driven

WHY IS DESIGN NECESSARY?

Multi-Platform

where will users be viewing the work?
